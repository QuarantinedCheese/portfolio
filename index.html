About me!

Hello! I am Cheese, currently an online Humber Polytechnic student, in my first year of the Computer Programming and Analysis program. I graduated from highschool, focusing on computer tech classes and advanced math. I have chosen to make this portfolio anonymous for now, because I highly value my online privacy.

I'd like to take my interest in computer software into my career in the future, preferably backend. At least when it comes to websites, I do not like doing frontend. I've been apart of a few coding competetions through the years but I've never placed anywhere too impressive.

Should I change my mind or this doesn't work out, I'd love to try and make writing a career, too! I've been practicing in any freetime I can gather, and I enjoy it a lot. It's fun to take random stories that pop into my head and put them into words. So far I haven't finished anything too long, but I'm still practicing!

I tend to use anthropomorphic characters in my writing because I find them more interesting and expressive, and it makes it easier to tell characters apart by sight description alone. I am not great on my knowledge of clothes, so this also makes it so that I can distract from that fact.

In highschool, I collaborated with Queens University to make a game that uses one of their motion-capturing systems! It is an endless runner, similar to the chrome dinosaur game, where you play a rogue robot stuck on mars, trying to escape!

Other than that, i'm interested biking and playing video games! The 'touch grass' meme is kind of funny to me, especially when I've just gotten back from a ride and I hop on a game to wind down, only to be told to 'touch grass' because I happen to be good. The main games I've been playing recently are: Brick Rigs, Rimworld, and Deep Rock Galactic.
